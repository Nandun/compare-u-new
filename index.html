CompareU

Interactive University Comparison Tool: CompareU. CompareU is a tool that will allow you to quickly compare attributes of universities from across the country. CompareU will pull information from the US Department of Education, and aggregate data on universities to provide a holistic view of the institution, such as its: tuition cost, acceptance rate, graduation rate, average starting salary, and more. It will allow you to visualize these comparisons using a radar chart, a visualization well suited to comparing entities across a number of different attributes. You could also click on a particular university to get more detailed information. Users who log in will be able to save their comparisons.
